Pimms

Ingredients

Item Quantity
Strawberries (chopped) 50
Cucumber (sliced) 1
Oranges (Sliced) 2
Fresh Mint Leaves A bunch
Pimm's No 1 50ml
Lemonade 150ml
Ice Plenty!

Recipe

  • Put the strawberries, cucumber, oranges, mint and ice in a jug
  • Pour in the pimms and lemonade (1 part pimms, 3 parts lemonade)
  • Stir, serve, and enjoy!
